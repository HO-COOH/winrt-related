## Syntax

```xml
<uap5:FileType>   
  A string with a value between 1 and 64 characters in length that must begin with a period ("."), cannot have additional periods, and cannot contain these characters: <, >, :, ", /, \, |, ?, or *.
</uap5:FileType>
```
